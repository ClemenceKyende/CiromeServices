Our Expertise, Your Advantage

At CIROME Services Limited, we deliver practical and professional solutions across a wide range of engineering and environmental disciplines.

Our consultancy services are grounded in technical excellence, regulatory compliance, and a deep commitment to sustainable development.

From engineering design and civil works to environmental assessments and audits, our team is equipped to support both public and private sector projects at every stage of the project lifecycle.

Every service we offer is guided by efficiency, quality, and environmental stewardship ensuring impactful and cost-effective outcomes for our clients.

Our Services

We offer a wide range of engineering, environmental, and construction solutions tailored for sustainable development.

Engineering Designs and Consultancy

Architectural and engineering designs, site analysis, structural and services drawings, and Bills of Quantities.

Environmental Impact Assessment/Studies (EIA/S)

Comprehensive EIA studies to evaluate environmental effects and guide sustainable project development.

Environmental Audits and Monitoring

Environmental evaluations and audits for ongoing projects to ensure compliance and sustainability.

Civil Engineering and Construction

Construction of residential, commercial, and office blocks; drainage systems; culverts; and general civil works.

Roadworks and Drainage Construction

Grading, paving, labor-based roadworks, construction and maintenance of road drainage and culverts.

Registration and Compliance

Registered with NEMA and NCA for EIA/EA, building works, water works, and mechanical engineering services.

Cleaner Production and Waste Minimization

We apply sustainable methods to reduce waste and resource use, ensuring efficiency, cost savings, and environmental compliance.

Repair, Renovation and Maintenance

Specialized in the upkeep, remodeling, and refurbishment of commercial, residential, and office buildings for lasting structural integrity.

Water Infrastructure Projects

Construction of water pans, tanks, and small dams to enhance water accessibility and promote sustainable water resource management.

General Engineering Works

Offering a wide range of technical and engineering services including fabrication, installations, and custom infrastructure support.

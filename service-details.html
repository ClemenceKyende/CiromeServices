1. Engineering Designs and Consultancy

We provide comprehensive architectural and engineering consultancy services including:

  • Site analysis and outline proposal preparation
  • Preliminary and detailed design work
  • Final architectural and structural drawings
  • Engineering services design and Bills of Quantities

These services provide a strong foundation for project implementation, offering insights into technical, financial, and logistical expectations for successful project delivery.

2. Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) Studies

We undertake EIA for proposed developments to assess potential environmental, social, and health impacts. Our approach includes:

  • Comprehensive impact prediction and mitigation strategies
  • Preparation and filing of EIA reports with NEMA for licensing
  • Development of Environmental Management Plans (EMP)
  • Stakeholder consultation and awareness sessions

Our team ensures that your projects align with sustainability principles and national regulations.

3. Environmental Audits and Monitoring

We conduct audits for existing projects to evaluate environmental compliance. Our services include:

  • Field audits and performance evaluations
  • Monitoring of project environmental impact
  • Compliance checks with NEMA and other regulatory bodies
  • Submission of audit reports for ongoing regulatory approval

4. Civil Engineering and Building Construction

Our construction services cover both commercial and residential projects. Key offerings include:

  • Construction of residential, office, and commercial buildings
  • Earthworks, structural concrete, paving, and drainage systems
  • Parking lot upgrades and perimeter walls
  • Project supervision and client collaboration throughout the process

We emphasize quality, timelines, and client satisfaction in every project.

5. Roadworks and Drainage Construction

We handle the design, grading, and construction of roads including:

  • Labour-based and machine-based road grading
  • Paving works with blocks or asphalt
  • Construction of culverts and stormwater drainage systems
  • Road rehabilitation and periodic maintenance

Our roadwork solutions ensure durability, accessibility, and proper water management systems.

6. Registration and Compliance

CIROME Services Limited is registered with key professional bodies including:

  • National Environment Management Authority (NEMA) for EIA/EA
  • National Construction Authority (NCA) in the following categories:
    • Buildings Works
    • Water Works
    • Mechanical Engineering Works

This ensures our eligibility to offer certified professional services across various sectors.

7. Cleaner Production and Waste Minimization

We apply sustainable techniques in all our projects to minimize waste and optimize inputs:

  • Material and energy efficiency assessments
  • Process improvement to reduce resource wastage
  • Compliance with green building and construction practices
  • Promotion of eco-friendly project execution methods

This approach reduces environmental impact and operational costs while increasing project efficiency.

8. Repair, Renovation and Maintenance

We offer expert services in the repair and renovation of structures including:

  • Structural repairs and upgrades
  • Interior and exterior renovations for homes and offices
  • Maintenance schedules for plumbing, electrical, and mechanical systems
  • Retrofitting of aging structures to meet modern standards

9. Water Infrastructure Projects

We specialize in developing water conservation and distribution solutions:

  • Construction of water tanks, dams, and water pans
  • Installation of rainwater harvesting systems
  • Drainage and irrigation channels
  • Water supply infrastructure for rural and urban areas

These solutions support sustainable water management in both agricultural and residential settings.

10. General Engineering Works

We handle a variety of specialized engineering tasks based on client needs:

  • Fabrication and installation of structural components
  • Mechanical repairs and custom metal works
  • Site preparation and utility installations
  • Support services for unique technical projects

Our flexible, expert team ensures timely execution and custom solutions across the board.

Who We Are

Driven by values, powered by engineering excellence

Explore Our Story

Shaping the Future with Engineering and Sustainability

CIROME Services Limited is a registered and incorporated company in Kenya, established in June 2021 to meet the growing demand for environmental and engineering solutions.

The world’s engineering challenges, particularly in developing countries, are ever-growing. With an increasing need for technical and professional support in development projects, we are here to provide that expertise.

Our commitment to sustainable development ensures that future generations can enjoy the benefits of today’s projects while minimizing environmental impact.

At CIROME Services, we specialize in providing consulting services in engineering and environmental management. With a team of highly experienced professionals, we offer effective and innovative engineering solutions.

Our Vision

We seek to be the company of choice in delivery of decent facilities with quality support services and systems.

  • To be the best Engineering and Environmental Consultancy Company of choice in Kenya by providing innovative and highly competitive technical performances.
  • To provide our services using current technologies to the full satisfaction of our customers.
